About Me

Jordan Kue was born in Harper Woods, Michigan and always loved being outdoors. His long loved passion always dealt with animals and nature, until around 6 years old, when he dicovered his love for fishing. Ever since moving to Palmetto, Florida in 2009, the love for aqautic culture was what he enjoyed the most. Being the only boy in his family, this let his adventures branch into finding his new hobbies, which include skateboarding, crypto currency, and meditating.

About a year ago marks the day Jordan wanted to take on the job of Internet marketing. Many sleepless nights, a load of Youtube videos, and many articles later Jordan finally hacked how to make money on the Internet. After realizing the power of Internet Jordan decided to find ways how to take advantage of this oppurtunity before the world goes through another drastic change. With that said, UCF coding bootcamp is where he hopes to learn and indulge himself in order to reach the next stepping stone in his life.
